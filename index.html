FAQ.

Look through the answers to the most popular questions of our customers.
Where can I download your application? We have Android and iOS versions of the FoodOrder app. You can download them in Google Play or App Store.
How do I find the restaurant I need on the list? The application has a convenient Quick Search section that will help you find any restaurant you need by its name or cuisine.
What if I don't see the restaurant I need on the list? If the restaurant you want is not listed, please contact customer support. We will connect it to our system shortly.
What if your courier will not arrive on time? If our courier hasn't delivered your order in 30 minutes, contact our support team. You will receive your order for free.
Do you have any special offers for your loyal clients? Yes. We regularly run all kinds of promotions and offer free delivery for customers who make more than 5 orders per month.
How can I pay for my order? Can I pay online? We offer different payment options. You can pay online in the app or by card when your order is submitted, or you can pay when the food is delivered.
